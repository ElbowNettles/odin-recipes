Puree Cube Pasta/ Eggless Pappa

Description

This tomato pasta is a vegan edit of the 'pappa' our Italian grandparents fed us all the time as kids (one of three courses!). It's a kind of pasta soup- the above pic is not an accurate representation. You will need a bowl.

Chicken stock and butter are replaced, beaten egg is left out, and spinach is added for health. Nonna used farfallini, sometimes stelline (my fave), but you can use or throw in whatever.

Ingredients

Serves One (generously)

  • 2tbsp(ish) Tomato Puree
  • 2 Oxo Cubes (vegetable or vegan chicken, ideally one of each)
  • 1tbsp(ish) Butter Alternative
  • 1 bag Spinach
  • 4 fistfuls(ish) Penne (or equivalent)

Instructions

  1. Mix puree, crumbled stock cubes, and butter in saucepan with approx. 1l water and bring to boil
  2. Add pasta, spinach, and enough water so that the pasta is entirely submerged
  3. Put pan lid on and simmer for 5-10mins, stirring occasionally, until pasta is al dente
  4. Buon Appetito!
