Toast

Description

You've probably had this one before, but if not- wow, you are in for a treat

Ingredients

  • Bread (sliced)

Recommended Toppings

  • Butter
  • Marmite
  • Tomatoes
  • Avocado
  • Beans
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Toast bread for two minutes, flipping halfway through
  2. Apply spreads such as butter, mayo, Marmite, chocolate spread
  3. Add solid toppings such as vegetables, fruits, plant-based proteins
  4. Drizzle with liquids such as oil, balsamic vinegar, raspberry coulis
  5. Slice diagonally to minimise crust corners
